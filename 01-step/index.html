Step 1

Add a app icon which will be shown on homesreen

First of all we need to create an icon. I used Medialoot's iPhone App Icon Kit to design one...

We need one icon for every possible resolution: <<= iPhone 3G 58x58px, >= iPhone 4 72x72px, iPad 1/2 114x114px, >= new iPad 144x144px). To let iOS know where to find your icons for the different resolutions simply add these lines to your <head>-Tag on your page.

<link rel="apple-touch-icon-precomposed" href="#{resource['icon:58.png']}" />
<link rel="apple-touch-icon-precomposed" sizes="72x72" href="#{resource['icon:72.png']}" />
<link rel="apple-touch-icon-precomposed" sizes="114x114" href="#{resource['icon:114.png']}" />
