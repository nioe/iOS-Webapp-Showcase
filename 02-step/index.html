Step 2

Remove Safari's address bar and prevent the user from zooming in and out on your page.

Simply add these to lines to the <head>-section of your application:

<meta name="apple-mobile-web-app-capable" content="yes" />
<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width; initial-scale=1.0; maximum-scale=1.0; user-scalable=no;" />

The first line will tell iOS that your application shall be interpreted as an Apple Mobile App - which means nothing more than that Safri will remove its controls (Back- and Forward-Button, Address-Bar, etc.).

When you add this page to your homescreen and open the newly created App you won't see Safari's controls anymore - Yeah! Have a look at your multitask overview screen. You'll notice that iOS treats your Webapp now also as an independent task.
